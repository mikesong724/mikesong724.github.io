I'm currently a machine learning quantitative researcher at JPMorgan Asset Management in NYC. I enjoy learning and conducting AI/ML research during my free time.

My research focus is primarily on graph machine learning. Specifically, I am interested in fair and robust graph learning and unsupervised/few-shot graph learning for biology/chemistry applications such as drug design, and protein structure prediction. So far, I am fortunate to be mentored by Prof. Jundong Li and Prof. Brian Pierce.

I am also a part-time Master's student in the CS department at Columbia University. I went to undergraduate school at University of Virginia and received my B.A. in 2019.
